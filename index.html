Meet The Owner

Kye is a versatile stylist whose love for all things beauty started when she took cosmetology in high school. Although she didn't get licensed right away, she reconnected with her love of the industry in 2017 when she began working at PR @ Partners in Warrenton as an apprentice. Kye received her cosmetology license in 2019 and ventured out to open her own salon in 2020! She is a color specialist who loves highlighting, blended balayages, and fashion/vivid color work. Kye also offers waxing services at her salon. As a curly girl herself, Kye loves working with naturally curly hair and helping to guide her clients in learning to work with their curls or smooth them out! If you're looking for a multiskilled stylist with a passion for her work, Kye is the perfect fit!
